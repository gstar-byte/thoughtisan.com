Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 26, 2026

At Lumi Note, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share your personal information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our colorful idea-capture and task management experience, we collect the following data:

  • Personal Authentication Data: When you register or log in, we collect your email address, display name, and profile picture url (if authenticated via Google).
  • Your Generated Content: We store all the notes, tags, categories, task checklists, visual color-codes, and reminders that you create in order to sync them securely across your devices.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide, personalize, and improve your note-taking experience.
  • Synchronize your notes and to-dos instantly across all your registered devices.
  • Verify account authentication and Premium subscription status.

We strictly **do not sell** your personal data or your note contents to any third parties under any circumstances.

3. Data Synchronization & Security

All your content and authentication profiles are securely stored and synced using Firebase Cloud Services (Firestore Database & Firebase Authentication). We utilize standard industry encryption protocols (HTTPS/SSL) to protect your data in transit and at rest.

4. Your Data Control & Ownership

You retain full ownership and complete control over your content. You can modify, archive, or permanently delete any capsules, tags, categories, and account information directly inside the application settings at any time with immediate effect.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, please feel free to reach out to us at hello@luminote.space. We will respond to your inquiry promptly.
