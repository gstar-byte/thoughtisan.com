Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 26, 2026

1. Information Collection

We collect minimal personal data required to provide a high-quality experience. This includes basic account info (username, email) and user content (notes, todos, categories, tags, reminders) created inside the application.

2. Information Use

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Securely synchronizing your personal thoughts and tasks to the cloud (via Google Firebase Firestore).
  • Personalizing UI theme layouts, cards colors, and application display states.
  • Fulfilling legal obligations as required by active law enforcement or official court procedures.

3. Data Sharing

Lumi Note does not sell, lease, or share your personal data with third-party companies. Your files are private and remain strictly under your control, accessible only under authorization.

4. Security Measures

We utilize robust industry-standard encryption protocols (SSL/TLS, HTTPS) to protect all information during transit and implement tight cloud database rules (Firebase Security Rules) to prevent unauthorized server breaches.

5. Your Rights

You reserve complete ownership over your data. You may request data deletion, profile modification, or full data exports at any time by contacting our support team at hello@luminote.space.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically to align with security advancements. Registered users will be informed of critical changes via email notifications or top banner updates.
